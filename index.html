Capitaly

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Capitaly ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal and business finance management application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data We Collect

Capitaly stores all financial data locally on your device and optionally syncs via Apple iCloud (CloudKit) if enabled. We do not operate our own servers to store your financial data.

  • Financial transactions (income, expenses, transfers) - stored locally and on iCloud
  • Client information (names, contact details) - stored locally and on iCloud
  • Wallet balances - stored locally and on iCloud
  • Goals, budgets, quotes - stored locally and on iCloud

3. Third-Party Services

Capitaly may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple iCloud (CloudKit) - For data sync between your devices. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In - Optional authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API - Optional AI features for transaction parsing. When you use AI features, the text you enter is sent to Anthropic's API. You must provide your own API key. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sheets API - Optional export feature. Only accesses sheets you explicitly authorize.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers
  • We do not have access to your financial data (it's stored on your device/iCloud)
  • We do not store your API keys on our servers

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework with encryption at rest. If you enable iCloud sync, data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. Biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) is available for additional security.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access - All your data is visible within the app
  • Export - You can export your data as CSV at any time
  • Delete - You can delete all data by removing the app. iCloud data can be deleted from Settings > Apple ID > iCloud
  • Portability - CSV export allows you to move your data anywhere

7. Children's Privacy

Capitaly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
